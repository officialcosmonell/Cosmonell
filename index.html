Cosmonell Brand Package

The acquisition of Cosmonell.com includes the transfer of matching official brand assets and an established digital presence, including Cosmonell Official profiles across X, Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube.

These official profiles were secured and created alongside the domain as part of a unified brand vision, remaining clean and reserved for future development. All associated accounts are connected to the dedicated official email address created specifically for the Cosmonell brand, which will also be transferred to the buyer as part of the acquisition process.

The package also includes a premium visual identity ecosystem featuring a high-end logo and a cinematic 6-second brand intro, designed for immediate use across websites, social platforms, presentations, and future media expansion.

Unified branding, visual consistency, and ready-to-use assets create a seamless global ecosystem prepared for immediate deployment and long-term growth.

All transfers will be conducted securely in coordination with Sedo during the transaction process.
